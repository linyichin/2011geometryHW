F_{yx} & F_{yy} & F_{yz} \\
F_{zx} & F_{zy} & F_{zz}\end{array}
\right)$ 的 adjoint Matrix, i.e. $A=\det(\partial^2F)(\partial^2F)^{-1}$
\end{problem}
